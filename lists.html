The Best Crockpot Ribs

Description

If you really want a no-mess, easy-to-make meal, Crockpot Ribs are the best!

Ingredients

  • Beef Ribs
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic

Directions

  1. Clean the ribs and season with garlic, salt and pepper and allow it to marinate overnight.
  2. Get a crockpot and plug it in. Set it to low.
  3. Place the ribs along with BBQ sauce into the crockpot cooker.
  4. Set the timer on for 10 hours on low heat.
  5. When finished, enjoy your fall-off-the-bones ribs!
